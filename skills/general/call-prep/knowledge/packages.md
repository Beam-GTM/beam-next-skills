# Beam AI — Repeatable Packages (Quick Reference)

## Qualifying Matrix

When a prospect describes their problem, map it to a cell:

|  | Classify & Route | Extract & Enter | Monitor & Chase | Evaluate & Decide |
|--|-----------------|----------------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Operations** | Email Triage, Ticket Classification, Returns Handling | Order Entry, Document Extraction | Vendor Onboarding | |
| **Finance** | | Invoice Creation, Quote Generation | AR Collections, AR Reconciliation, FP&A Chasing | Expense Validation, KYC Verification |
| **HR / Talent** | | | | CV Screening, Candidate Qualification |

**30-second qualifying:**
1. **What arrives?** Email, ticket, document, or system event
2. **What happens to it?** Classify/route, extract/enter, monitor/chase, or evaluate/decide
3. **What function?** Operations, finance, or HR

## The 8 Packages

### 1. Email Triage & Routing
**Sell to:** Head of Ops, 5K+ emails/month shared inbox
**Proof:** Linde (6K emails/month, 90% spam recall) · Wünsche (8K emails/year, German) · Rivertrace (certificates)

### 2. Ticket Classification & Response
**Sell to:** Head of CX/Support, high ticket volume
**Proof:** Limehome (11K units, production) · FINN (Zendesk, completed) · T2 (bilingual Arabic+English) · 1NCE (30+ countries)

### 3. Order Entry
**Sell to:** Head of Sales Ops, orders arriving by email/PDF
**Proof:** Fraisa (91% accuracy, 1-5 min vs 4-15 min human) · Coolback (3 branches, 20 edge cases)

### 4. Document Extraction
**Sell to:** Head of Ops/QA, manual PDF-to-spreadsheet work
**Proof:** Sunday Natural (46 fields, 12 doc types, 95% confidence) · Moverii (4 parallel agents)

### 5. AR Collections
**Sell to:** Head of Finance/AR Manager, manual payment chasing
**Proof:** BID-Coburg (94.4% accuracy, **€5.2M/year savings**) · Smartly (SOX compliant) · Betterment (80-120 hrs/month saved)

### 6. CV Screening
**Sell to:** Head of Talent/HR, high application volume
**Proof:** Booth & Partners (**18,951 screenings**, 97.78% completion, 70% faster) · Favorite Staffing (10K candidates/month)

### 7. Expense Validation
**Sell to:** Head of Shared Services/Finance Ops, travel expense volume
**Proof:** MHP Porsche (46 rules, **6-15 seconds/report**, replaces 5.5 FTEs)

### 8. FP&A Report Consolidation
**Sell to:** CFO/FP&A Lead, multi-BU monthly reporting
**Proof:** Americana Foods (19 factories, target >=90% accuracy, >=80% effort reduction)
