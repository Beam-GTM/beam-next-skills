# /brainstorm

Generate a burst of ideas quickly without filtering.

## Usage

User provides:
- **Topic or challenge** — what to brainstorm about
- **Quantity** (optional) — how many ideas (default: 10)
- **Constraints** (optional) — any limitations to consider

## Workflow

### Step 1: Clarify the Challenge

Reframe as a "How might we..." question:
- "I need blog topics" → "How might we create content that drives engagement?"
- "Marketing ideas" → "How might we reach [audience] with [message]?"

### Step 2: Generate Ideas Across Categories

Use multiple lenses:
- **Conventional**: What's the obvious answer?
- **Opposite**: What if we did the reverse?
- **Extreme**: What's the 10x version?
- **Steal**: What works in other industries?
- **Combine**: What two things could merge?
- **Simplify**: What's the minimum viable version?

### Step 3: Present Ideas

Format:
```
## Ideas for: [challenge]

### Quick Wins (easy to implement)
1. Idea — brief rationale
2. Idea — brief rationale
3. Idea — brief rationale

### Bold Moves (higher effort, bigger impact)
4. Idea — brief rationale
5. Idea — brief rationale
6. Idea — brief rationale

### Wild Cards (unconventional, worth exploring)
7. Idea — brief rationale
8. Idea — brief rationale
9. Idea — brief rationale
10. Idea — brief rationale
```

## Brainstorm Boosters

When stuck, try:
- **What would [person] do?** (Elon Musk, your competitor, a child)
- **What if money wasn't a constraint?**
- **What if we had to launch tomorrow?**
- **What would make this 10x better?**
- **What would the opposite approach look like?**

## Example

**Challenge**: "Ideas for team offsite activities"

**Ideas**:

### Quick Wins
1. **Escape room** — forces collaboration under pressure
2. **Lightning talks** — each person teaches something non-work
3. **Walking meeting** — explore the neighborhood, no screens

### Bold Moves
4. **Hackathon** — build something fun in 4 hours, present at dinner
5. **Customer visit** — meet actual users, hear their stories
6. **Skill swap** — pair up, teach each other something new

### Wild Cards
7. **Silent retreat morning** — 2 hours of individual reflection, then share insights
8. **Reverse offsite** — customers/partners join for part of it
9. **Documentary night** — watch something industry-relevant, discuss
10. **Future newspaper** — write the headline we want to see in 1 year
