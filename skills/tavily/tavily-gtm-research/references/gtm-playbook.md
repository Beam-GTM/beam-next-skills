## Account Executives (AEs)

### Pre-Call Research Workflow

**Time Investment:** 5 minutes (vs. 25 minutes traditional)

**Step 1: Company Overview**
```
Search Tavily for "[Company Name] company overview 2026"
```
- Get recent company news
- Understand business model
- Identify key initiatives

**Step 2: Industry Context**
```
Search Tavily for "[Company Name] [industry] challenges 2026"
```
- Understand industry-specific pain points
- Identify market trends affecting them
- Find regulatory or compliance issues

**Step 3: Tech Stack Research**
```
Search Tavily for "[Company Name] tech stack"
Search Tavily for "[Company Name] technology partners"
```
- Identify integration opportunities
- Understand their current solutions
- Find replacement opportunities

**Step 4: Competitive Landscape**
```
Search Tavily for "[Company Name] competitors"
```
- Understand their competitive position
- Identify differentiation opportunities
- Find market positioning insights

**Output:** Comprehensive account brief ready for call

## Time-Saving Strategies

### Batch Research Sessions

**Instead of:** Researching each prospect individually
**Do:** Batch similar research tasks

**Example:**
```
1. Research 5 prospects in same industry at once
2. Search industry trends once, apply to all
3. Find common pain points
4. Personalize final 10% per prospect
```

**Time Savings:** 60% reduction

---

### Create Research Templates

**Build reusable search sequences for:**
- Pre-call research
- Competitive analysis
- Industry trends
- Technical validation

**Save as:** Notion templates, Slack saved messages, or text snippets

---

### Leverage Domain Filtering

**Instead of:** Sorting through low-quality results
**Do:** Pre-filter to authoritative sources

**Example:**
```
Search Tavily for "statistics" only from statista.com and census.gov
```

**Time Savings:** 40% reduction in result review time

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ❌ Too Vague
"Search Tavily for AI" → No useful results

### ✅ Specific
"Search Tavily for AI automation in finance teams 2026" → Great results

### ❌ No Time Context
"Search Tavily for trends" → Outdated info

### ✅ Time-Bound
"Search Tavily for trends 2026" → Current info

### ❌ Ignoring Domain Quality
Accepting all results → Wasted time on low-quality sources

### ✅ Filter by Authority
Using domain filters → High-quality sources only
