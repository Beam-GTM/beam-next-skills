# /voice-check

Verify content matches a specific brand voice or personal style.

## Usage

User provides:
- **Content to check** — draft to evaluate
- **Voice reference** (optional) — whose voice to match, or use corrections.md patterns

## Workflow

### Step 1: Identify Voice Markers

Check for common mismatches:

| Dimension | Corporate Trap | Authentic Voice |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Opening | "I am pleased to announce..." | "We just shipped..." |
| Jargon | "leverage synergies" | "work together" |
| Perspective | Third person, passive | First person, active |
| Confidence | "We believe that perhaps..." | "Here's what works:" |
| Engagement | "What do you think? Comment below!" | Specific question or none |

### Step 2: Score Voice Match

Rate 1-10 on:
- **Authenticity**: Does it sound human or templated?
- **Consistency**: Does it match past content?
- **Specificity**: Concrete details vs generic statements?
- **Energy**: Active voice vs passive constructions?

### Step 3: Flag Issues & Suggest Fixes

For each issue found:
- Quote the problematic phrase
- Explain why it's off-brand
- Suggest alternative phrasing

## Quick Voice Patterns

**LinkedIn (professional but human)**:
- Start with insight, not announcement
- Use "I" and "we" naturally
- Specific examples > abstract claims
- End with question OR nothing (never forced CTA)

**Email (direct, respectful)**:
- Get to the point in first sentence
- Clear ask or next step
- Warm but not overly casual

**Documentation (clear, scannable)**:
- Short sentences
- Bullet points for lists
- Code examples where relevant
- No marketing speak

## Example

**Draft**: "We are excited to announce that our team has been working hard to deliver a new feature that will help you be more productive."

**Voice Check Results**:
- ❌ "We are excited to announce" — corporate opener, sounds like a press release
- ❌ "working hard" — filler, doesn't add value
- ❌ "help you be more productive" — vague benefit claim

**Suggested Rewrite**: "New feature: [X] is live. Here's how it saves you 30 minutes per day on [specific task]."
