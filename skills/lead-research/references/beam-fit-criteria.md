# Beam Fit Criteria

Use this to assess whether a prospect fits Beam's focus.

## Core Positioning

> "We automate outsourced work for large enterprises. Our AI agents replace entire job functions — not just assist, but actually do the work."

## Target Market: Outsourced Work Automation

Beam focuses on the **$500B BPO/RPO market** — any work companies outsource to:
- Business Process Outsourcers (BPOs)
- Recruiting Process Outsourcers (RPOs)
- Shared services centers
- External providers

## Ideal Use Cases

### Strong Fit ✅

| Characteristic | Why It Fits |
|----------------|-------------|
| **High volume** | Thousands of tasks/documents per month |
| **Repetitive structure** | Same process, different inputs |
| **Clear inputs/outputs** | Documents in → structured data out |
| **Manual labor today** | Humans doing repetitive work |
| **Quality-critical** | Errors are costly, consistency matters |
| **Currently outsourced** | Already paying external providers |

### Adjacent Fit ⚠️ (Worth Exploring)

| Characteristic | Consideration |
|----------------|---------------|
| **Internal ops** | If it's BPO-style work done internally |
| **Document processing** | Data extraction, structuring, transformation |
| **Back-office operations** | Finance, HR, admin processes |

### Poor Fit ❌

| Characteristic | Why Not |
|----------------|---------|
| **One-off projects** | Not repeatable |
| **Highly creative work** | Low structure, high judgment |
| **Low volume** | Automation doesn't pay off |
| **Customer-facing AI** | Chatbots, support (not our focus) |
| **Internal analytics** | Business intelligence, dashboards |

## Proof Points by Use Case

### Finance/Debt Collection
- **BID-Coburg:** 88% accuracy, 500k+ monthly tasks, €25k→€1.5M (60x growth)

### Insurance Processing
- **Zurich:** 26-country rollout potential

### HR/Recruiting (BPO/RPO)
- **Booth & Partners:** CV screening, 70% faster, 9,533 candidates, $15k→$216k (14x)

### Automotive/Enterprise
- **VW/Porsche:** 4-hour deployment, self-learning (34%→75% accuracy)

## Key Differentiators

| Differentiator | Message |
|----------------|---------|
| **Speed to production** | 9 months → 4 weeks → 4 hours (getting faster) |
| **Self-learning** | Agents improve autonomously without prompt engineering |
| **Replace, not assist** | Full job function automation, not copilots |
| **Integration depth** | Deep system connections create switching costs |

## Qualification Questions

### Volume & Scale
- How many [tasks/documents/candidates] per month?
- How many people work on this today?
- Is this work currently outsourced?

### Pain Signals
- What's the biggest bottleneck? (speed, cost, quality, people)
- Have you tried automation before? What happened?
- What would 50% faster / 70% cheaper mean for you?

### Decision Signals
- Is there budget allocated?
- Who else needs to be involved?
- What would a successful pilot look like?

## Red Flags

| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| "Just exploring" with no budget | Tire-kicking |
| Vague problem definition | Haven't thought it through |
| Need corporate approval for everything | Slow decision cycle |
| Want custom solution, not platform | Consultancy trap |
| Low volume (<100/month) | ROI won't work |

## Green Lights

| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Specific problem with metrics | Real pain |
| Budget mentioned or implied | Serious buyer |
| "Can we do a pilot?" | Ready to move |
| Decision-maker on the call | Fast cycle |
| Currently outsourcing this work | Budget already exists |
| High volume (1000s/month) | Strong ROI case |
