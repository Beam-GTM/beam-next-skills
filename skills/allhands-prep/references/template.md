## 4. The Ask (3 mins)

**Speaker:** [Main presenter name]

### For Engineering 🛠️

> "You're the team with the most capacity and the least direct customer contact. That's about to change. I want you to feel connected to *why* we're building what we're building. When you ship something, I want you to know which customer it's for and what it unlocks for them. We're going to find more ways to make that connection real."

### For Solutions 🌉

> "You're the bridge. You translate customer needs into product reality. When you see a gap, raise it fast. When engineering ships something great, help sales tell that story."

### For Product 🔄

> "Your job is to spot the patterns. When we solve something great for one customer, you figure out how to make it work for the next ten. You turn custom solutions into repeatable features. That's how we scale without losing the magic."

### For GTM 🎯

> "You're the front line. Keep bringing the customer voice back to us — the wins, the frustrations, the opportunities. And when solutions and engineering deliver for your customer, tell them. Celebrate the team effort."

### For Everyone 🤝

> "Support each other. If someone's blocked, help unblock them. If someone needs context, share it. We win together or we don't win at all."
